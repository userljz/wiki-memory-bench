## Known Limitations

- These are manual, cost-bounded smoke evaluations. They are not default CI and they are not final leaderboard claims.
- Results depend on provider behavior, caching, model choice, prompt stability, and any local OpenAI-compatible endpoint configuration.
- `deterministic judge` means answer quality is being calibrated with LLM answerers while keeping correctness scoring deterministic.
- Prompt artifacts are saved under each run's `artifacts/llm/answerer/` directory so prompt/response details can be audited later.
