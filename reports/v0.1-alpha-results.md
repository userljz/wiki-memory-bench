## Interpretation Notes

- These results are benchmark artifacts for research and development, not product claims.
- Poor-performing rows are intentionally kept in the table. No rows are removed to improve presentation.
- Deterministic baselines are useful for reproducibility but may understate or overstate what a stronger learned answerer could do.
